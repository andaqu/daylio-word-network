Upload your dailyo entries in CSV format as to build your word network in a 'force-directed graph'. A node represents a word. An edge between two noddes implies that two words are significantly (*) used together. The colour of any node represent the average mood for a given word when used in conjuction with its connected words. Stop-words can also be filtered out.
